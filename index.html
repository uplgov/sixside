A Land of Ancient Peaks and Hidden Paths

Journey into Sixside, a land shaped by ancient mountains, dense forests, and winding streams. Here, tradition and nature intertwine, guiding those who seek its profound depths.

About Sixside

History

Sixside's history is etched into its rugged landscape — a testament to generations who have lived in harmony with its formidable terrain. Deep valleys, isolated settlements, and enduring traditions define its resilient identity.

Geography

Towering mountains, bamboo forests, vines, and pristine streams. Its six irregular boundaries, shaped by natural contours, reveal themselves one by one to those who venture within — giving the province its name.

Population

The people of Sixside are as integral to its landscape as the mountains themselves. They navigate hidden paths, preserve ancient ways, and live in deep connection with the land.

Government Agencies

HL Trust

An independent organization dedicated to natural and cultural heritage. Receives ~44% of its annual funding from the Sixside provincial government. Oversees sustainable management of mineral and forest resources.

Sixside Revenue Authority (SRA)

Manages fiscal revenue through an incentive-based tax system — subsidizing health, sustainability, and community well-being, while discouraging harmful consumption.
