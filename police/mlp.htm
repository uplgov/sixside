Highway Patrol Division

SSPD Highway and Road Patrol Division is a core law enforcement entity within the state's traffic safety and public order framework. It is responsible for patrolling highways, expressways, main arteries, and interregional routes, as well as oversight and emergency response. The division's primary mission is to ensure road safety, maintain public order, and enhance emergency response capabilities, guaranteeing stable, orderly, and efficient road network operations under both daily and contingency circumstances.

The Highway and Road Patrol enforces traffic laws on key segments, prosecutes serious violations including speeding, reckless driving, DUI, and illegal modifications. Officers respond to traffic collisions as first responders, directing traffic, securing scenes, conducting preliminary investigations, and coordinating rescue efforts. They serve as the frontline force in road safety management, vital to preserving public safety on state roads.

For cross‑regional pursuits, major incident responses, or rapid mobility support, the Highway and Road Patrol performs coordinated tasks. Acting under official directives, they cooperate with other law enforcement branches to track suspect vehicles, contain routes, provide real‑time road intelligence, and assist interdiction operations, ensuring the safety and effectiveness of enforcement activities. Familiarity with road environments and high‑mobility equipment makes the division indispensable in inter‑agency cooperation.

Additionally, the division conducts road inspections, traffic infrastructure hazard assessments, traffic security for major events, and public education campaigns. It provides continuous support for the long‑term stability of the state's transportation system. The division operates under principles of professionalism, consistency, and efficiency, striving to create a safe, orderly road environment for the public.

SSPD Fleet Inventory

"Inspect the beasts that run on asphalt — up close."

The SSPD periodically acquires eligible vehicles from the market, whether brand‑new or pre‑owned. These vehicles are subsequently refurbished or modified by authorised third‑party contractors. As a result, our fleet comprises a wide variety of models. This website displays only the most common types currently in service. You are welcome to arrange a visit to the SSPD car park, where you may view and photograph the vehicles in person.
